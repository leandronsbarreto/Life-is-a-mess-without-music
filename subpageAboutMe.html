About me

My name is Leandro Barreto, I am 35 years old and come from Brazil. Since 2016 I have been living in Germany, where I study and work. Two years ago I started my Master's in Media Science at University Bonn. This year I will finish the course. I have a student job at the IT department of a big company for advertising in Germany. Although I love music, I can't unfortunately play any musical instrument. I hope you enjoy my blog.
